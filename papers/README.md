# Downloaded Papers

## Core Papers on Cross-lingual Embedding Similarity

1. **A Survey of Cross-lingual Word Embedding Models** (Ruder et al., 2019)
   - File: `ruder2017_survey_crosslingual_embeddings.pdf`
   - arXiv: 1706.04902 | Citations: 566
   - Comprehensive survey and typology of CLWE methods

2. **Unsupervised Cross-lingual Representation Learning at Scale** (Conneau et al., 2020)
   - File: `conneau2020_xlmr.pdf`
   - arXiv: 1911.02116 | Citations: 7836
   - XLM-R: state-of-the-art multilingual model

3. **How Multilingual is Multilingual BERT?** (Pires et al., 2019)
   - File: `pires2019_multilingual_bert.pdf`
   - arXiv: 2004.09813 | Citations: 1605
   - Empirical study of mBERT's cross-lingual abilities

4. **Emerging Cross-lingual Structure in Pretrained Language Models** (Wu & Conneau, 2020)
   - File: `chi2020_emerging_crosslingual.pdf`
   - arXiv: 1911.01464 | Citations: 300
   - Shows universal latent symmetries in embedding spaces (DEEP READ)

5. **A Primer in BERTology** (Rogers et al., 2020)
   - File: `rogers2020_bertology.pdf`
   - arXiv: 2004.01401 | Citations: 1757
   - Survey of what BERT learns and how it works

## Language Neutrality and Alignment

6. **On the Language Neutrality of Pre-trained Multilingual Representations** (Libovicky et al., 2020)
   - File: `libovicky2020_language_neutrality.pdf`
   - arXiv: 2005.00396 | Citations: 115
   - Shows mBERT is NOT language-neutral; centering helps

7. **How Language-Neutral is Multilingual BERT?** (Wu & Dredze, 2019)
   - File: `wu2019_language_neutral_mbert.pdf`
   - arXiv: 1906.01502 | Citations: 127
   - Probes language-specific vs. language-neutral representations

8. **First Align, then Predict** (Dufter & Schutze, 2021)
   - File: `dufter2021_first_align_predict.pdf`
   - arXiv: 2101.11112 | Citations: 86
   - Understanding cross-lingual ability of mBERT

9. **Cross-lingual Alignment Methods for Multilingual BERT** (Cao et al., 2020)
   - File: `cao2020_alignment_methods.pdf`
   - arXiv: 2003.08134 | Citations: 51
   - Comparative study of alignment methods

10. **Unsupervised Multilingual Word Embeddings** (Chen & Cardie, 2018)
    - File: `chen2018_unsupervised_multilingual.pdf`
    - arXiv: 1809.05053 | Citations: 138
    - Unsupervised CLWE exploiting language interdependencies

## Polysemy and Multi-sense Embeddings

11. **Beyond Bilingual: Multi-sense Word Embeddings using Multilingual Context** (Upadhyay et al., 2017)
    - File: `upadhyay2017_multisense_multilingual.pdf`
    - Source: RepL4NLP 2017 | Citations: 27
    - Multilingual signals for word sense disambiguation in embeddings (DEEP READ)

12. **SensEmBERT: Context-Enhanced Sense Embeddings for Multilingual WSD** (Scarlini et al., 2020)
    - File: `scarlini2020_sensembert.pdf`
    - arXiv: 2003.06865 | Citations: 121
    - Sense embeddings from BERT for multilingual WSD

13. **Cross-Lingual Contextual Word Embeddings Mapping With Multi-Sense Words In Mind** (Zhang et al., 2019)
    - File: `zhang2019_crosslingual_multisense.pdf`
    - arXiv: 1910.10811 | Citations: 4
    - Multi-sense words as noise in cross-lingual alignment
